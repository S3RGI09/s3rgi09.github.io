Projects

On this website I will list all the projects I have planned and already completed in order from most to least developed.

To contact me you can send me an email: s3rgi09@iname.com

  • CyberForecast: This project is a cybersecurity start-up, web security is very expensive and CyberForecast aims to bring it to the whole world at much lower costs with the existing infrastructure and services of other companies. CyberForecast Web

  • DoDay.: DoDay is an app specifically designed for teenagers that promotes connection through group "adventures." Through creative and fun challenges, users can plan activities with friends, encouraging quality time in real life. Each adventure becomes the perfect excuse to go out, experience, and create memories while sharing and documenting their journey through photos and videos. DoDay. Web

  • Contributions to Debian: Debian is a completely free software Linux operating system. It is maintained by the community, so I spend a lot of time contributing to Debian. Companies and organizations such as Google, Valve, Wikipedia and CERN use Debian for its stability and active, altruistic community. Debian Web

  • Contributions to FreeBSD: FreeBSD is one of the most important operating systems today, it is used by companies like Netflix for their CND Open Connect, Meta for their servers, Play Station with the OS of their consoles, even MacOS is based on BSD. That's why I contribute to this system, the community is simply lovely and programming to help sustain the highly digitalized world is a fascinating task. FreeBSD Web

  • Contributions to Nmap Project: Nmap is the most popular tool among hackers and one of the most necessary, its port scanner is essential for audits and its scripts and other tools such as Ncat or Ncrack are necessary for network management, maintenance and security. Nmap project Web
